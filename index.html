How I work

Before I open Figma.

01
Strategic

I start with the business model, the CEO's vision, and the market. Before any design decision I need to understand what success looks like — for the company and for the user.

02
Research

I conduct real user research — one-on-one interviews, empathy mapping, pattern recognition. I find the human truth that everything should be built on. This step cannot be skipped.

03
Execution

I prototype fast, test rigorously with real users, and iterate until the solution is right. I move from insight to screen with precision — protecting the human truth all the way to launch.

My methodology is not a process imposed on your team. It is a way of breathing — it becomes part of your culture.

Creative Director & UX Strategist

I design from strategy to screen. Before opening Figma I immerse myself in the business model, the CEO's vision, and deep user research — finding the human truth that everything should be built on.

I have spent 10+ years building from scratch in startup environments — at Zyper (acquired by Discord), DataSwift (Cambridge), Pearmill, and through my own agency Pixel People Co. I have scaled a business to 58 cities, contributed to two acquisitions, and grown a publication to 3,000 daily readers.

I am drawn to AI products that serve human wellbeing — specifically the question of how you make emotionally intelligent technology feel safe, trustworthy, and genuinely human to a real person in a vulnerable moment. That is the design problem I have spent my career solving.
